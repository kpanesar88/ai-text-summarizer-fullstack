How to Get Started with Quick Extract

Ready to see the magic of AI in action? Follow these simple steps to start summarizing your text effortlessly:

Step 1: Enter Your Text

Just type or paste the text you want to summarize into the provided input area.

Step 2: Hit "Summarize"

Click the "Summarize" button to let our AI get to work and generate a concise summary.

Step 3: Review the Summary

Check out the summary and make any tweaks you need to ensure it captures all the key points.

Step 4: Copy or Save

Copy the summary to your clipboard or save it for future reference. It’s that simple!
